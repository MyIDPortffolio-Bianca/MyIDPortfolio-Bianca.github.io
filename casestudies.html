01

AI as Collaborator: Designing a Theology Unit from Scratch

A live simulation documenting how I use AI as a genuine thinking partner — not a content generator — to navigate complex, culturally sensitive curriculum design. Starting from a completely blank slate, I designed a six-week first-year theology unit exploring the intersection of Catholic Social Teaching and Indigenous Australian spirituality. The simulation demonstrates professional judgement, design process, and critical approach to AI-assisted practice — including moments where I corrected, challenged, and redirected AI output using my own expertise.

03

Reflective Writing Coach: Psychology Unit Cogniti Agent

A real challenge surfaced by the academic team — students arriving without the skills to write reflectively. This AI-powered agent responds by guiding students through established reflection frameworks including Gibbs' Reflective Cycle, adapting to what each student wants to explore. Students choose their own framework or let the agent recommend one, then receive structured guidance and feedback to develop their writing.
